CCTV CAMERAS INSTALLATIONS

Monitoring

The use of physical access controls and monitoring personnel and equipment entering and leaving as well as
auditing/logging all physical events are primary elements in maintaining overall organizational security.

Cameras

Cameras are normally integrated into the overall security program and centrally monitored.
Cameras provide a flexible method of surveillance and monitoring.
They can be a deterrent to criminal activity, can detect activities if combined with other
sensors and, if recorded, can provide evidence after the activity. They are often used in locations where
access is difficult or there is a need for a forensic record.
